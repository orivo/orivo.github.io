About US

Orivo international was established with the moto to spread the richness and purity of Indian agriculture across the globe. The Ayurveda contains humungous wealth of knowledge on health. We, at orivo international, pick some treasure from the rich source of Ayurvedic herbs , grown organically keeping their medicinal and traditional value in mind. We strive to become the India’s leading name in the field of Herbs, Oil Products and cereals all grown 100% organically with certifications from the government and universally recognised certifying agencies with the commitment to understand the clients’ requirements complying with today’s stringent and competitive international market conditions.

Orivo international offers highest quality of Aromatic Indian basmati and non-basmati organic and conventional rice of multiple varieties. Our decade old pool of trusted and certified farmers ensures the consistent supply of quality rice.

Consumer health and customer satisfaction together with good and sustainable agricultural practices are the foundation of our business values .
